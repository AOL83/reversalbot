# Code of Conduct

We pledge to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project users.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take appropriate action.

## Reporting
Report incidents to maintainers via the security email listed in SECURITY.md.
